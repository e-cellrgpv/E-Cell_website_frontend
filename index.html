    ABOUT SIP

    The start-up internship program is a part of Entrepreneurship Cell, RGPV conceptualized for the students who are delighted, committed, and inquisitive to know more about the start-up community and aims at starting their own start-up or comprehends about pursuing a career in the start-up community. This program provides the gateway for the college students to make them cognizant about the start-up industry and help them do internships in the start-up companies..

    OUR GOAL

    SIP targets are providing internships to a maximum number of students and giving them the required exposure and experience. This will make interns more enthusiastic towards startups and make them capable of learning new things each day. SIP will help in developing innovation with intellectual resources to bring out the best entrepreneurial leader in you.
